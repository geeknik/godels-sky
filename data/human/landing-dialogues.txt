# Gödel's Sky Consequence-Based Landing Phrases
# These phrases provide flavor text when landing on planets, reflecting
# the player's reputation, behavior pattern, and relationships.



# POSITIVE REPUTATION LANDING PHRASES

phrase "consequence landing: hero welcome"
	word
		"The spaceport workers wave as you land. Your reputation precedes you."
		"A small crowd has gathered to watch your ship touch down. Word of your deeds has spread."
		"The landing controller sounds genuinely pleased to guide you in. 'Welcome back, Captain.'"
		"Port officials meet you at the hatch with warm greetings. You're known here."
		"A young pilot asks for your autograph as you disembark. Your reputation has grown."

phrase "consequence landing: trader welcome"
	word
		"Merchants are already approaching as your cargo bay opens. They know your reputation for fair dealing."
		"The harbormaster greets you personally. 'Always good to see reliable traders,' they say."
		"Several shipping companies have left messages requesting your services. Word spreads about good captains."
		"Your preferred docking berth has been held for you. The port remembers its best customers."
		"A trade delegation is waiting at the dock. Your reputation for honest commerce draws interest."

phrase "consequence landing: protector honor"
	word
		"A family approaches with thanks. You saved their relative's ship once. They never forgot."
		"The local militia salutes as you land. Protectors are honored here."
		"Someone you once rescued is working at the spaceport. They wave gratefully as you pass."
		"Children point excitedly at your ship. They've heard stories about the captain who helps people."
		"A memorial plaque near the dock lists ships you've saved. Your name is remembered."



# NEGATIVE REPUTATION LANDING PHRASES

phrase "consequence landing: cold reception"
	word
		"The spaceport workers avoid eye contact as you land. Your reputation precedes you."
		"Security personnel watch you carefully as you disembark. They've heard stories."
		"The landing controller's voice is clipped and professional. No warmth here."
		"People give you a wide berth as you walk through the port. Whispers follow you."
		"A merchant quickly closes their stall as you approach. They don't want your business."

phrase "consequence landing: hostile territory"
	word
		"Armed security meets you at the dock. 'We're watching you,' they say flatly."
		"Your landing permission came with conditions. No weapons displayed. No extended stay."
		"The port has assigned you a berth far from the main terminal. They want you isolated."
		"A protest group has gathered near your ship. They haven't forgotten what you did."
		"The local news is covering your arrival. The coverage is not flattering."

phrase "consequence landing: pirate presence"
	word
		"Legitimate merchants quickly move away as you approach. Your reputation is known."
		"Security scans your ship extra carefully. They're looking for evidence."
		"A bounty hunter watches you from across the dock, making notes."
		"Port officials demand additional documentation. They're making things difficult."
		"Insurance representatives are already calculating premiums based on your presence."



# BEHAVIOR PATTERN PHRASES

phrase "consequence landing: bounty hunter"
	word
		"Other captains eye your ship with respect. They know a hunter when they see one."
		"A wanted poster on the wall matches someone you eliminated. Good work pays off."
		"The local law enforcement nods respectfully. They appreciate your kind of help."
		"A criminal recognizes you and quickly leaves the port. Your reputation precedes you."
		"Bounty postings are updated as you land. They know you're in the market."

phrase "consequence landing: warmonger fear"
	word
		"The port clears rapidly as you land. People remember what you're capable of."
		"Armed guards take positions around your ship. They're not sure what you'll do."
		"The landing controller's voice trembles slightly. They've heard the stories."
		"Civilian ships request priority departure as you arrive. No one wants to be near you."
		"Emergency protocols activate automatically. Your reputation triggers them."



# WITNESS-AWARE PHRASES

phrase "consequence landing: watched"
	word
		"You notice surveillance drones tracking your ship as you land."
		"Security cameras follow your movements from the moment you touch down."
		"An undercover agent is poorly hidden in the crowd. You're being observed."
		"Your ship is scanned multiple times during landing. They're thorough here."
		"A government vehicle is parked near your berth. Someone official is interested."

phrase "consequence landing: ghost"
	word
		"You land without fanfare. No one pays special attention. Just another ship."
		"The port processes you routinely. Your reputation here is... unremarkable."
		"Security gives you a cursory glance and moves on. Nothing to see here."
		"You blend into the crowd of captains. Anonymous and unremarked."
		"The bureaucracy processes you efficiently. Just another number in the system."



# FLEET SIZE PHRASES

phrase "consequence landing: major operation"
	word
		"Your fleet fills multiple berths. Port officials scramble to accommodate you."
		"The harbormaster personally oversees your landing. A fleet this size demands attention."
		"Other captains watch with a mix of envy and concern as your ships dock."
		"Fuel and supply vendors compete for your business. Fleet operations are lucrative."
		"The port adjusts traffic patterns to accommodate your arrival. You're a major operation now."

phrase "consequence landing: lone wolf"
	word
		"Your single ship slots neatly into a standard berth. Efficient and unassuming."
		"The port processes you quickly. Solo operators are easy to handle."
		"You appreciate the simplicity of landing one ship. No coordination headaches."
		"Other fleet commanders look down on your solo operation. You don't mind."
		"The maintenance crew has your ship serviced quickly. One ship, one job."



# FACTION-SPECIFIC PHRASES

		"Official Republic channels play a brief message thanking loyal captains like you."
		"A Republic recruitment officer approaches, then recognizes you. 'Never mind - you're already one of us.'"
		"The efficiency of Republic port operations reminds you why you support them."

		"Your landing clearance comes with warnings and restrictions."
		"The port assigns you the worst berth available. A message is being sent."

		"The Syndicate logo on the port reminds you of profitable partnerships."
		"Executive-level services are available to you. Your credit rating matters here."
		"Business proposals arrive before you've even left your ship. They know opportunity."

	word

		"Syndicate services are available, but at premium rates. Hostiles pay more."
		"Your accounts are flagged for additional verification. Nothing is easy here."
		"The corporate efficiency that usually helps now works against you."
		"Legal representatives are already preparing documentation. They're thorough."

	word
		"The Free Worlds welcome feels genuine, not bureaucratic. You're among friends."
		"Citizens wave hello as you dock. Community matters here."
		"The volunteer port workers seem genuinely happy to help. This is what freedom looks like."
		"Someone thanks you for supporting the cause. Your contributions are remembered."
		"The Free Worlds flag represents everything you've fought for. It feels like home."

	word
		"The normally friendly Free Worlds atmosphere feels cold. You've betrayed their trust."
		"Citizens turn away as you pass. Word of your actions has spread."
		"The volunteer spirit of Free Worlds ports doesn't extend to you."
		"Militia members watch you carefully. You're not a friend here anymore."
		"The freedom they celebrate doesn't feel like it includes you."
