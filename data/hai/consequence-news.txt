# HAI TRADER PATTERN REACTIONS
# =============================================================================

news "hai trader praise"
	to show
		"actionlog: pattern" == 1
	location
		government "Hai"
	name
		word
			"Hai merchant"
			"Hai trader"
			"Peaceful Hai"
	message
		word
			`"`
		word
			"The human captain brings good trade to our systems. Nut prices stable, no conflict. This is how visitors should behave."
			"My family's nut grove has prospered since this trader began visiting our systems. Fair prices, reliable schedule. We trust them."
			"Other humans attack. This captain only trades. Their presence is welcome in Hai space. We hope they continue."
			"The human understands balance. Trading enough to make profit, not so much as to disrupt markets. Respectful of our ways."
			"My grandfather said humans always bring trouble. This captain proves him wrong. Trade, not conquest. Cooperation, not conflict."
		word
			`"`
